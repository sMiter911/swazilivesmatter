Swazi Lives Matter

The Swazi Lives Matter Global Solidarity Movement (SLMGSM) is an international movement of Swazis and non-Swazis who coordinate and lead a global campaign to support democracy and freedom in Swaziland.

It was formed in 2021 in response to the massacre of Swazis in the June uprising, primarily to amplify the calls for democracy in Swaziland to the world.

The founding of the SLMGSM was a result of a growing conviction that in order to create a strong civil society that could work actively for democratization and poverty eradication, there would have to be more unity and coordination among all the well-meaning friends of Swaziland inside and outside of Swaziland.

It was important to ensure that the Swazi diaspora has a voice in the progressive movement of the country.

Our Vision

Solidarity

Maximize the cooperation, solidarity, both within and outside of Swaziland.

Sanctions

Isolate the oppressive ruling regime of Swaziland (tinkhundla) inside and outside the country.

Learning

Strengthen democratic struggles by sharing the lessons across the region and the continent.
